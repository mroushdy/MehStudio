# Marwan's mounting photo drop (2026-07-24, 16 images, described from the conversation)
All observations by direct inspection this session; confidence "inferred (photo)" unless

## The shared mounting language across every build shown
- ONE ROUND MOUNTING LAND PER DRIVER: a flat circular land (raised boss or routed
  recess) sized to the driver frame OD, with a CONCENTRIC center tap opening and its
  own screw circle (4-8 fasteners). Appears in wood (routed) and print (integrated).
- MIDS get OVAL/racetrack recessed lands with TWIN KIDNEY/OBROUND SLOTS through the
  bottom (wood cinema build, white printed pod, JMOD-manual style); WOOFERS get plain
  round cutouts (direct wall mount) or round lands with a single center round tap; CD
  gets a dedicated round flange (2/3/4-bolt) with a shaped throat (round -> clover /
  rect slot).
- ONE-PIECE vs SEPARATE: printed builds INTEGRATE the land into the horn/pod body
  (green pod: drivers bolt straight onto printed round faces; tan funnel: funnel base
  IS the flange; white corner pod: square CD land + oval mid recesses in one print).
  Wood builds use SEPARATE plates/boards (SH-96 corner boards; cinema build panels;
  Fusion CAD shows separate round disks with their own screw circles).
- SEALING (printed): the purple build shows an O-RING seated in a printed groove
  around the CD throat + brass HEAT-SET INSERTS (gold, 2 visible). Wood: felt/gasket
  pads (black felt on walls of the square 4x10 build).
- FASTENERS seen: brass heat-set inserts (purple, clear, green prints), machine
  screws into inserts, wood screws/T-nut-class on ply, captive M5 (JMOD manual page).
- ORIENTATION: magnet-out everywhere; no dedicated magnet brace beyond chamber walls.

## What this settles for pins 3/9 (subject to the workflow synthesis concurring)
- Pin 3 ("mounting plate should match driver"): every example sizes the land to the
  DRIVER'S OWN frame OD with a concentric tap - the land is driver-derived, never a
  generic disk.
- Pin 9 ("ring should connect as one piece to the big horn"): the PRINTED record
  agrees with him - integrated one-piece lands everywhere in print; the separate
  disk survives only as (a) wood plates/boards and (b) the printed CD PLATE (clear
  horn) where service access matters.

## SH-96 provenance + mounting facts (the source-hunt agent, sourced URLs in study 4)
- The interior photo = Danley's own InfoComm 2008 display unit (Flickr beavizzle
  2619651027). Woofers are 15in (my earlier 12in read was wrong - cone tops only).
- Official spec: 45W x 26.5H x 25D in, 4x15 LF + 6x4 MF + 1x1.4 HF, 90x60. Mids
  2008 press said 6x5 - flag the 4-vs-5 discrepancy.
- Official no-grill render: woofer vents = ROUNDED SLOTS IN THE HORN CORNERS.
- diyaudio Droco photo-analysis (post-5841736): the woofer "only has its edge
  placed over the tap, not its center" - TAP AT THE CONE EDGE, not concentric,
  for the SH-96 corner woofers; "vented backchamber" per post-5841738.
